FAQ

Answers to Common Questions

Here are some common questions I get asked. Feel free to reach out if you have more!

What is your design process like?

My design process starts with understanding your goals and users, followed by research, wireframing, prototyping, and iterative testing. I ensure constant communication to align with your vision and deliver user-centric designs.

How long does a project take?

Project timelines depend on scope, but typically range from 1 weeks for small projects to 2-3 weeks or more for larger ones.

Do you work with startups?

Sure! I love helping startups bring their ideas to life with user-focused designs and robust development.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes, I offer maintenance and support packages to ensure your website or app remains updated and performs optimally. We can discuss a plan that suits your needs after project completion.

My company doesn’t have a brand logo yet, can you help with that?

Absolutely! I’d be happy to create a custom brand logo for your business.

Can you also create video ads for my brand?

Yes, I can help with that too! We can discuss the pricing details based on your specific needs.
